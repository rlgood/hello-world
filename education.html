Education

In 2008 I had the opportunity to be the first person in my family to pursue a 4 year college degree. At eighteen I wasn't really sure what I wanted to do for the rest of my life, nothing stood out to me. The University of Wisconsin-Stevens Point checked a lot boxes, it was close enough to family that I could still see them on the weekends but it was far enough away that it seemed exotic. It was also very affordable compared to other options I was considering. In 2012 I graduation with a double major in Economics and Business Administration

In 2016 Sentry Insurance saw the need for data analysts and donated $4,000,000 to UW-Stevens Point to fund the Data Analytics program. The availablilty of such a great program available from my Alma Mater made UW-Stevens Point the clear choice for when I returned to University in 2020.
